My Hobbies

I love DIY crafts of all kinds. I draw and paint in a lot of mediums. I love Martial Arts and have a second degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do.
I also love music and play the drums. I have an 11 piece drumset that I hop on every once in awhile. I also have two Congo African Grey parrots
that I have raised from birth.

Skippy and Scookie

These boys turned 16 on the 4th of January. I've had them since they were 3-4 weeks old. They talk just like you and I. They know how to count, their ABC's and colors. They will ask to watch a movie, and their favorite ones are Rio, Cars, Stewart Little and anything to do with American Idol. They also pray for everything and everyone before going to bed at night.
